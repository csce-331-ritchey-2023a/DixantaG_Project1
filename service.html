Sophmores Progressing in Excellence and Sustainability

During my time with SPIES, I worked with my peers to work towards a goal of a cleaner and more Sustainable enviorment for the Bryan-College Station area. We held events such as Slolympics which was a competition for the Sophmore leadership organizations here on campus. We did things from planning events and designing shirts. We worked on park and street clean ups. In these clean ups we looked for all types of trash that could be picked up, but it was also another way for us to bond with eachother. Another similar event was the Big event which is a one day project to help the residents of Bryan-College Station. We were each given a goal to achive for the residents, and as a team we would get it done by the end of the day. This was a great way to give back to the community as a thank you. We also promoted better and cleaner methods to add to people's lives.
